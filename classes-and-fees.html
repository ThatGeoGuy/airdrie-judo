Class Breakdown

Pre-Judo — Introduction to Judo

  • For pre-kindergarden & kindergarden ages (4 - 6 years old).
  • 3 classes, choose one per week, 45 minute classes.
  • Limit of 10 students per class.
  • No Judo Gi (uniform) required.
  • Athlete receives a t-shirt.

Recreational Judo — Judo for life

  • For all athletes 7 to 12 years of age.
  • 2 classes, choose one per week, 1 hour classes.
  • Limit of 24 athletes per class.
  • Athlete receives a white Gi.

Junior Competitive Program — Judo as a martial sport

  • For all athletes 8 and older.
  • 1 class, twice per week, 1 hour 30 minute classes.
  • Will travel and compete within Alberta and possibly Western Canada.
  • No maximum number of athletes.
  • Athlete receives a blue or white Gi.

Senior Competitive Program — National competitive Judo

  • For all athletes 12 and older.
  • 1 class, four times per week, 1 hour 45 minute classes.
  • Will travel for tournaments and training camps across Canada and possibly internationally.
  • No maximum number of athletes.
  • Athlete receives a blue or white Gi.
